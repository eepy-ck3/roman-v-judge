How the Bet Score Works

The "Bet Score" is a weighted formula designed to mirror how MVP voters actually think:


fWAR · 25%
The holy grail. Captures everything — offense, defense, baserunning. MVP voters use it even if they call it "eye test."
wRC+ · 20%
Park and league-adjusted offensive production. 100 = league avg. 170 = stupid good.
HR · 18%
Voters LOVE dingers. Judge won his MVP by hitting 62. Unfair? Sure. Reality? Also yes.
OPS · 15%
Classic power + patience combo. Easy to understand, hard to fake.
RBI · 10%
Context-dependent, kinda unfair, but voters care and we're not pretending otherwise.
Runs + SB · 12%
Rounds out the offensive picture. Roman is faster — this is where he gains ground.

Each stat is compared between the two players. The winner's share of the "total value" in each category gets weighted and summed. Scores always add up to 100.
